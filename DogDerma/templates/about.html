About DogDerma

DogDerma is an AI-powered web application designed to help pet owners identify potential skin conditions in their dogs. Using advanced machine learning techniques, our tool can analyze images of dog skin to detect common conditions such as dermatitis, ringworm, demodicosis, and more.

This application was developed as an educational project to demonstrate the capabilities of AI in veterinary medicine. While it can provide preliminary insights, it is not meant to replace professional veterinary care.

Our goal is to provide a useful tool that can help dog owners become more aware of their pet's skin health and seek appropriate care sooner when issues arise.

How It Works

DogDerma uses a deep learning model trained on thousands of images of dog skin conditions. The model is based on ResNet50 architecture, a type of convolutional neural network (CNN) that is highly effective at image classification tasks.

When you upload an image of your dog's skin, the application:

  1. Preprocesses the image to standardize its format
  2. Runs the image through our trained neural network
  3. Analyzes the output to determine the most likely condition
  4. Provides results with confidence scores and educational information

The model can identify six categories of skin conditions, with each identification accompanied by information about the condition and general treatment approaches.
