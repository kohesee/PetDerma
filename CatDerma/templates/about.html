About CatDerma

CatDerma is an AI-powered tool designed to help cat owners and veterinarians identify potential skin diseases in cats. Using advanced deep learning algorithms, CatDerma can analyze images of cat skin and provide insights about possible conditions, helping to catch issues early and guide treatment decisions.

Our mission is to improve feline health by making skin disease detection more accessible and providing educational resources about common skin conditions that affect cats.

How It Works

Upload an Image

Take a clear, well-lit photo of your cat's skin condition and upload it to our secure platform.

AI Analysis

Our machine learning model, trained on thousands of cat skin images, analyzes the photo to identify patterns associated with common skin conditions.

Get Insights

Receive detailed information about potential conditions, including descriptions and general treatment approaches.

Veterinary Consultation

Use these insights as a starting point for discussions with your veterinarian, who can provide proper diagnosis and treatment.

Our Technology

CatDerma uses a state-of-the-art ResNet50 deep learning model that has been specifically trained on a diverse dataset of cat skin conditions. The model can identify the following conditions:

  • Flea Allergy Dermatitis - An allergic reaction to flea saliva
  • Ringworm - A common fungal infection
  • Scabies - A skin condition caused by mites
  • Healthy Skin - Normal, disease-free skin

While our AI model is highly accurate, it's important to remember that it should be used as a screening tool rather than a replacement for professional veterinary care.

Important Disclaimer

CatDerma is designed to be an educational and screening tool only. The information provided by this application should not be considered medical advice and is not intended to replace consultation with a qualified veterinarian.

Always consult with a veterinarian for proper diagnosis and treatment of any health issues affecting your cat. Early intervention is key to managing many skin conditions effectively.
